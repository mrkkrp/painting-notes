# Holbein 1130-40

The palette has 13 wells. All colors from Daniel Smith, the color chart:
<https://janeblundellart.blogspot.com/2017/04/daniel-smith-watercolour-full-range.html>

* Hansa yellow medium
* Hansa yellow deep
* Quinacridone gold
* Raw sienna
* Pyrrol scarlet
* Indian red
* Burnt sienna
* Burnt umber
* Cerulean blue
* Cobalt blue
* Ultramarine blue
* Phthalo blue (GS)
* Neutral tint
